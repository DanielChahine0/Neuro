# Neuro

Stay focused. Build better habits.

Neuro is a macOS menu bar app that helps you take control of your attention. It tracks your work sessions, detects distractions in real time, and blocks the apps that pull you off task — so you can actually get things done.

> Coming soon for macOS 14+ · Free during beta

---

## Features

- **Work Sessions** — Start timed deep work sessions with real-time activity monitoring. Neuro tracks which apps you use and flags when you drift off-task.
- **Distraction Detection** — Every distraction is logged automatically — which apps and websites pulled your focus, and exactly how long you spent on each.
- **App Blocking** — Block distracting apps and websites during focus sessions. Once a session starts, blocked apps can't be opened until it's over.
- **Focus Score** — A real-time percentage showing how productive vs. distracted your session was. Watch your score improve over time.
- **Stats & Streaks** — Weekly and monthly charts tracking your hours, distraction counts, and focus trends. Build streaks to stay consistent day after day.
- **Social Accountability** — Invite friends, see who's in a session, view each other's logged time, and send congratulations when they finish.

---

## How It Works

1. Start a focus session from the menu bar
2. Neuro monitors your active apps in the background
3. Distracting apps you've blocked are prevented from opening
4. At the end of your session, see your Focus Score and a full breakdown of where your time went
5. Track your progress over days and weeks — and keep your streak alive

---

## Platform

- macOS 14 (Sonoma) or later
- Lives in your menu bar — no clutter, always accessible
